Executive Summary

The rapid evolution of Generative AI is reshaping expectations in higher education, yet most university systems remain fragmented—separating academics, wellbeing, and career development into disconnected platforms.

The Problem

Students lack a unified, personalized support system that can guide them academically, emotionally, and professionally. This fragmentation results in:

  • Inconsistent academic performance
  • Poor course selection and planning
  • Disconnected mental health support
  • Limited career preparedness

Problem Context & Research

The Challenge

Higher education is undergoing significant transformation as universities attempt to integrate emerging GenAI technologies into traditional academic structures. Most universities rely on fragmented systems that separately manage courses, wellbeing services, and career support.

Core Problem

Students do not receive integrated, timely, and personalized support across academics, wellbeing, and career development, leading to:

  • Irregular academic performance
  • Unnecessary stress
  • Inefficient course planning
  • Limited preparedness for industry roles

Key Stakeholders

Students

Struggle with navigating multiple systems and lack actionable insights into academic trajectory

Faculty

Face pressure to provide individualized feedback while managing grading and administration

Advisors

Require holistic student views for course selection and progress monitoring

Administration

Aim to enhance retention rates and support student wellbeing

Key Research Findings

  • Existing LMS Limitations: Current platforms are primarily administrative—lacking deep personalization and behavioral insights
  • Holistic Data Importance: Combining academic metrics with wellbeing indicators creates more accurate student representations
  • Early Intervention Value: Students benefit significantly from early warnings and personalized recommendations
  • Market Demand: Strong evidence supports demand for adaptive learning tools and AI-powered academic coaching

Proposed Solution

ForgeEd is a unified, AI-enhanced Learning Management System designed to support students holistically across academics, wellbeing, and career development.

Core Functional Modules

1

AI-Powered Learning & Tutoring (SLU GPT)

  • 24/7 academic support and personalized explanations
  • Study strategies and contextual guidance
  • Based on enrolled courses and career goals
  • Rich text formatting (headers, lists, code blocks)
2

90-Day Career Learning Roadmap

  • Structured, day-locked learning plan
  • AI-produced content and theory explanations
  • Curated external resources
  • Automated daily proficiency checks
3

Dual Quiz System

  • Academic Quiz: 15 questions from active courses
  • Career Learning Quiz: 10 questions on roadmap topic
  • AI auto-scoring with personalized feedback
  • Historical tracking and performance trends
4

Wellbeing & Engagement Tracking

  • Daily wellbeing indicators via slider interface
  • Metrics: Happiness, Stress, Energy, Motivation, Sleep
  • Early pattern detection for disengagement
  • AI-generated motivational insights
5

Predictive Analytics Dashboard

  • Consolidated GPA, risk levels, quiz metrics
  • Interactive charts tracking performance trends
  • Actionable insights for improvement
  • Personalized recommendations
6

Admin Management Tools

  • Manage courses and generate roadmaps
  • Monitor at-risk students
  • Aggregated data visualization
  • Program-level analytics

Database Design

Entity Relationship Diagram

Core Data Entities

STUDENTS

User profiles with credentials, academic metrics (GPA, completion rate), career goals, and background

COURSES

Course catalog with codes, names, faculty information, and intake terms

ENROLLED_COURSES

Junction table tracking student course enrollment, progress, grades, and modules completed

DAILY_ROADMAP

90-day personalized career learning path with day-locked topics and resources

CAREER_QUIZ_HISTORY

Historical records of Career Learning Quiz attempts with scores and AI feedback

ACADEMIC_QUIZ_HISTORY

Historical records of Academic Quiz attempts with performance data

WELLBEING_ASSESSMENTS

Daily wellbeing check-ins tracking happiness, stress, energy, motivation, sleep quality

SLU_GPT_CHAT_HISTORY

Chat conversation history maintaining context for personalized AI tutoring

Data Relationships

  • Students have many Enrolled Courses and Daily Roadmaps (1:N)
  • Students have many Quiz History records and Wellbeing Assessments (1:N)
  • Courses have many Enrolled Students (1:N)
  • All entities connect to create a unified student profile

Core Features

Academic Features

  • Student Dashboard: Track GPA, completion rate, Career Quiz performance, Academic Quiz performance, and Wellbeing scores
  • Career Learning System: 90-day personalized learning roadmap with day-locking mechanism
  • Dual Quiz System: Career Learning Quiz (10 questions) and Academic Quiz (15 questions) with AI-powered auto-scoring
  • Course Management: Enrollment tracking, progress monitoring, and AI-powered course recommendations

AI-Powered Features

  • Performance Analysis: Personalized insights based on academic metrics
  • SLU GPT Chat Assistant: Context-aware conversational AI with rich text formatting
  • Course Recommendations: Based on career goals and educational background
  • 90-Day Roadmap Generation: Personalized learning paths
  • Theory Content Generation: AI-produced content for each roadmap topic
  • Wellbeing Insights: AI-generated motivational support

Wellbeing & Analytics

  • Wellbeing Tracking: Daily mental health assessments with emoji sliders
  • Performance Analytics: Interactive Chart.js line graphs
  • Predictive Risk Assessment: Early detection of academic and emotional risk
  • Admin Monitoring: Comprehensive admin interface for student performance tracking

Admin Features

  • Manage Students: Add/delete students with automatic roadmap generation
  • Manage Courses: Add/delete courses from the catalog
  • Student Monitoring: View comprehensive student performance metrics
  • Risk Alerts: Identify at-risk students for intervention
